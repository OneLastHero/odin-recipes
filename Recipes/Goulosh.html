When you're not in the mood for spaghetting try this delicious dish!

You can prepare it with different type of cheese and different pasta's

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Pasta
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Ground Pepper, Salt, Oregano, and Garlic Salt

    • Cooking Instrucitons

    1. Boil Pasta
    2. Cook Ground Beef and Season
    3. Heat up pasta
    4. Heat up Sauce

      5. Once everything is cooked, place beef in the pasta and the sauce. Mix ingrending well and cover, then allow it to sit for 10 minutes and serve.
